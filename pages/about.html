Who I Am

I am a passionate and dedicated Computer Science student at Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka (UTeM), specializing in Software Development with a CGPA of 3.57/4.0. My journey in technology has been driven by curiosity and the desire to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between hardware and software.

Throughout my academic career, I have developed a deep interest in IoT systems, mobile application development, and embedded systems. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives. My approach combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving, resulting in projects that are both functional and impactful.

What drives me is the opportunity to work on projects that combine multiple disciplines. Whether it's designing circuits for IoT devices, developing mobile applications with seamless user experiences, or creating web dashboards for real-time data visualization, I thrive in environments where I can apply diverse skills to create comprehensive solutions.
